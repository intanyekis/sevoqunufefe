Beyonce Wears $2 Million in Diamonds at the Brit Awards: Pictures

Now that is some major bling, Beyonce! The “XO” singer performed her latest hit single at the 2014 Brit Awards on Wednesday, Feb. 19, in London wearing a whopping $2 million in Lorraine Schwartz diamonds.

Twinkling onstage, Beyonce shined bright in a figure-hugging, teal, Swarovski crystal-coated gown by Vrettos Vrettakos — the same designer who created her bedazzled blue bodysuit for the Mrs. Carter World Tour.
