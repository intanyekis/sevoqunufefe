Koel Mallick Wiki - Lifestyle-Career 

Early Life: - Koel was born in Kolkata to Bengali cinema star Ranjit Mallick and Deepa Mallick of Kolkata's famed Mallick Bari of Bhowanipore, i.e., the Mallick family, which is closely related to the strong Barat and Gupta families and is famous for its Durga Puja. Koel graduated from Modern High School for Girls and earned a BSc (honors) in psychology from Gokhale Memorial Girls' College, a University of Calcutta affiliated college.

Professional Career: - Mallick made his film debut in Nater Guru, alongside Jeet. Nater Guru was a huge box-office triumph. Mallick's debut in Tollywood came with the release of the film in 2003. She also received her first award from the 4th Tele Cine Awards in 2004 for Best Actress in a Leading Role Female for Nater Guru. Mallick plays Monica, a 23-year-old female in Nater Guru, which stars Jeet as the male protagonist. Since the release of Nater Guru, the team has appeared in eleven films together.

Mallick made her television debut as a celebrity competitor on Zee Bangla's Didi No. 1 in 2010. Katha O Kahini, her first talk show, was also hosted by her. The show was transferred to the channel Star Jalsha. Koel returned to television in 2013 as a celebrity judge on the Bengali version of Jhalak Dikhhla Jaa, with famed Bollywood choreographer Remo D'Souza. ETV Bangla showed the show. She also appeared as a judge on Dance Bangla Dance Junior and as a celebrity judge in Season 11. She has also been in Zee Bangla reality series like Rannaghor, Dadagiri Unlimited, and Didi No. 1.
