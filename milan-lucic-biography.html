Milan Lucic Bio (Wiki)

Who is Milan Lucic?

Milan Lucic is a Canadian professional ice hockey left winger and an alternate captain for the Edmonton Oilers of the NHL.

Previously, he played for Boston Bruins and Los Angeles Kings.

Milan Lucic: Age, Parents, Siblings, Nationality, Ethnicity

He was born on June 7, 1988, in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada. Milan is currently 34 years old.

He was born to Serbian couple Dobrivoje “Dobro” Lučić and Snežana Kesa.

His father is a longshoreman who emigrated from Yugoslavia and his mother arrived in Yugoslavia with her family at the age of 2.

He has an older brother named Jovan and a younger brother named Nikola.

Milan holds Canadian citizenship but his ethnicity is unknown.

Education, School/College University

For his education, he joined Killarney Secondary in Vancouver and became a fan of the hometown Vancouver Canucks. Later, he joined South Delta Secondary School.

He was diagnosed with Scheuermann’s disease at the age of 15.

Milan Lucic: Professional Life and Career

In 2004, Milan Lucic started his career and played with Coquitlam Express and Vancouver Giants. He was selected by Boston Bruins in the Entry Draft.

On October 5, 200, he debuted his NHL career with Boston Bruins and was later chosen as the Giants’ next team captain and played there until the 2013 -2014 season.

Then, Milan was traded to the Los Angeles Kings on June 26, 2015, and played the season with 20 goals.

By the following year, he became an unrestricted free agent and signed with Edmonton Oilers with a Severn year, $42 million contract.

His MVP performance was at the 2007 Memorial Cup and he became the team captain of Canada in 2007 for the 2007 Super Series against Russia.

He rose to fame even more for a hit against opposing goaltender Ryan Miller during a game against the Buffalo Sabres.

He is at present associated with a team named Calgary Flames.

Awards, Nominations

He won President’s Cup, NHL Young Stars Game, the Stanley Cup champion, Seventh Player Award Memorial Cup, Stafford Smythe Memorial Trophy, and Eddie Shore Award.

Milan Lucic: Net Worth, Salary

He has an estimated net worth of around $22.5 million as of 2022, and he has earned that sum of money from his professional career.

Also, he earns around $3 million per year in his career.

Milan Lucic: Rumors and Controversy

There was a rumor swirling on Vancouver radio and was talking about a potential Milan Lucic for Loui Eriksson trade, and if Jesse Puljujarvi could be included in it.

Body Measurements: Height, Weight

Milan Lucic has a height of 6 ft. 4 inches and weighs 105 kg.

He has dark brown eyes and brown hair.
