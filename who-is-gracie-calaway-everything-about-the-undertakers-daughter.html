Who Is Gracie Calaway’s Mother?

Gracie Calaway’s mother is Sara Calaway (born Sara Chirie Frank), an American professional wrestler and wrestling valet. Sara discovered her passion for wrestling while in high school and was fondly nicknamed The Strong One by her classmates who noticed her strength.

Despite her early exposure to wrestling, Sara only became a professional wrestler with her debut at the 2001 King of the Ring. Furthermore, an alleged stalker released several footages of her wrestling and this also contributed to her rise to fame. Sara also appeared as a valet for her ex-husband The Undertaker in 2001 and the 2002 WWE.

What Happened To Gracie Calaway’s Parents?

Calaway’s parents are divorced. The celebrity couple who got married on 21 July 2000 seemingly had a loving marriage. During their time together, her mother Sara served as a valet to The Undertaker and also made public appearances with him at events. There are also videos showing the couple wrestling together and all of these made the news of their divorce shocking to their fans.

While they never disclosed the reason for their divorce, it was alleged that The Undertaker had cheated on his wife with WWE Diva Michelle McCool and this put a dent in their happy union. Dracie Calaway’s parents eventually settled for a divorce in 2007 after just seven years together. Barely three years later, The Undertaker went ahead to marry Michelle, giving credibility to the cheating rumors.

The divorce notwithstanding, Gracie’s parents have remained good friends and have jointly raised the two children they had together. The Undertaker had tattooed his ex-wife Sara’s name on his throat as a wedding gift to her and after their divorce, he re-inked it to make it look like a sketchy chain.
