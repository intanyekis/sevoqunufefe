Graham Wardle: A Canadian Actor and Filmmaker with a $3 Million Net Worth

Graham Wardle, a Canadian actor, filmmaker, and photographer, has a net worth of $3 million. He was born in Mission, British Columbia, in September 1986 and accidentally auditioned for a toy commercial, which led to his first role in the TV series The Sentinel in 1998.

Wardle is best known for his role as Ty Borden in the television series Heartland. He has also starred in several movies, including In the Land of Women, That One Night, Yesterday, and Mon Ami. Along with acting, he has produced three films and is a co-founder of the Lone Maverick Movie and Entertainment Company.

Wardle has been nominated for a Leo Award for Best Lead Performance by a Male in a Dramatic Series for Heartland in 2009, 2010, and 2012. He has also appeared in episodes of popular TV series such as The New Addams Family, The Outer Limits, Life as We Know It, and Supernatural.
