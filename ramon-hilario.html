How did Ramon Hilario start his Professional Career?

Hilario made his acting debut as Carlos in the 2003 short film Querida Josefina. During his early years, he appeared in Los Desaparecidos, Jimmy Kimmel Live!, and Malcolm in the Middle. Finally, in 2004, the American actor rose to prominence after playing Papi in the episode On Tilt of The Shield.

Among the films in which he appeared were Confession, Dream, The Inside, In Justice, Diamond Lane, Lynx, Saving Grace, and Happy Hour. His other acting credits include How to Be a Serial Killer, Rita Rocks, Paradise Drive, Raincheck Romance, How I Met Your Mother, Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D., Modern Family, Games People Play, Kidding, Bob Hearts Abishola, and B Positive.
