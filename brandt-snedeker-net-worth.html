Brandt Snedeker is an American professional golfer with an estimated net worth of $20 million.

Brandt Snedeker learned about golf through his maternal grandmother who managed a golf course in Missouri. In 2003, he won the U.S. Amateur Public Links.

He turned pro in 2004 during the Nationwide Tour. He was hailed as the PGA Tour Rookie of the Year in 2007 and FedEx Cup Champion winner in 2012. Snedeker competed in several tournaments such as Wyndham Championship, Hyundai Tournament of Champions, and Farmer Insurance Open.

Brandt Newell Snedeker was born on December 8, 1980 in Nashville, Tennessee.
