Gisele Bundchen Goes Completely Nude, Bares Her Butt for Vogue Paris

Stripping down! Gisele Bundchen is helping Vogue Paris celebrate their 95th anniversary by going completely nude — well, almost completely. In the French publication’s celebratory issue, the model took it all off, minus one black, pleather knee-high boot.

The photo shoot by famed photographer Mario Testino (who also happens to be one of Bundchen’s buds) also features the Brazilian beauty, 35, in an unzipped Moschino dress (that teases a peek of her famous glutes) and topless, in nothing but a pair of boots and underwear that read, “Gisele.”

Clearly excited about her part in the French fashion mag’s anniversary issue, the former Victoria’s Secret Angel—who has topped Forbes’ list of the highest paid models since 2002—shared a sneak peek of some of the racy images via Instagram on Thursday, Sept. 24.
