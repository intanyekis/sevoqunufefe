Who Is Tuki Brando?

Tuki Brando is the grandson of Marlon  Brandon the oscar-winning actor. He was born on June 26 1990 in Taiwan. Tuki is the grandson of Oscar-winning actor, Marlon Brando and his last wife, Tarita Teripaia.  He is currently 33 years old.

His mother, Cheyenne Brando was the second child of Marlon and Tarita’s union. He grew up with his maternal grandmother in Tahiti. His mother lost custody of him due to her mental instability. He became an orphan at the age of five.

Tuki not only hid behind the shadows of his famous family members but he also took his own path in life as a model. 7. Marlon Brando Left No Arrangement For His Tetiaroa Atoll

One of the great spots in Brando’s life was spent with friends and family on his Tetiaroa Island Atoll in Tahiti. He discovered the tiny piece of heaven while scouting for locations for Mutiny on the Boat in 1960.  After obtaining the land, he built a village on the islets and used it as a vacationing spot.

Two years to his death, he signed a new will that left out Tetiaroa out. He had leased the islets for 99 years. Presently, a Brando resort was built there by the Tetiaroa Beachcomber SC. It opened in 2014 and all his eleven children are involved in running the place.
