The Game's 8-year-old daughter's confessed who her '1st love' is

The Game is known for being one of the most iconic rappers of the West Coast hip-hop scene in the past years. He is the father of three children, and his youngest daughter is growing up to be a model.

California "Cali" Lynn Dream Taylor is a gorgeous 8-year-old that is not shy of being under the spotlight. Her Instagram account is managed by her parents and is filled with her best modeling pictures and videos.

A while ago, a photo of The Game kissing his little girl's cheek was uploaded to her account. The adorable pic was captioned:

"Daddy will always be my 1st love."

Fans couldn't help but feel the tenderness of the little girl’s message and commented on how cute they looked together.

“ Love their relationship. She’s an adorable little girl.”

“You are his first love too, baby.”

“The best dad ever. The cutest thing I might ever see from a rapper.”

“Always the first love and the men who set our future standards. Cute”

Cali Lynn Dream Taylor has already secured modeling contracts and has a big fan base with over 178k followers on Instagram.

She's the youngest daughter of the rapper and ex-fiancé Tiffney Cambridge. The couple were together for over eight years and even documented their journey to the altar in the VH1's reality show "Marrying the Game" from 2012 to 2014. However, they ended up calling off their engagement because of the rapper's infidelities.

He admitted on Wendy Williams' show a few years ago that his lifestyle ruined the relationship, saying:

“I was a dog. I had infidelity issues and I blew it. It’s pretty much my fault because I had a good woman. She’s a school teacher, [has a] master’s degree, mother of my children [son King Justice, and daughter California Dream]. You don’t really get it when you’re in the relationship. You get out, she kicks you to the curb and then you realize what a great woman you had.”

The former flames have a good relationship despite their bitter end, and they are now focused on co-parenting their kids together in a loving environment.
