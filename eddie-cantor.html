Biography

Eddie Cantor was born in New York City, NY on January 31, 1892. Comedian, radio star, and songwriter who made his debut on Broadway in the Ziegfeld Follies of 1917 revue. He released hits like “Ma! He’s Makin’ Eyes at Me” and “Ida.” Doris Day recorded a version of Cantor’s hit song “Makin’ Whoopee.” Due to a misunderstanding while signing up for school, his last name was recorded as Kanter instead of Kantrowitz.
