Kevin Harts Revelation of Testing Positive for Coronavirus


Earlier this year, Kevin Hart disclosed that he had tested positive for Coronavirus.

The 41-year-old comedian and actor made this revelation during his performance at the latest edition of Dave Chappelle’s An Intimate Socially Distanced Affair show in Yellow Springs, Ohio over the weekend.

According to Page Six, Kevin shared his experience with the virus, stating, ‘The problem is that I had it around the same time as Tom Hanks, and I couldn’t say anything because he’s more famous than I am.’

Tom Hanks and his wife Rita Wilson had announced their positive COVID-19 tests on March 11, but have since recovered from the illness. 

A source revealed to Page Six that Chappelle had spent $100,000 on coronavirus rapid testing for attendees of the outdoor shows. The lineup of other comics included Questlove, Tobe Nwigwe, Bill Burr, Michelle Wolf, and Donnell Rawlings.
