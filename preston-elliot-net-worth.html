Preston Elliot is an American radio personality, disc jockey, and anchor with an estimated net worth of $4 million. Born as Preston O'Neil Wilson at Missouri in the year 1968, Elliot is presently based in Philadelphia. Preston dreamed of being a rock star but took to the radio when he found himself lacking in what it really took to be one.

The Preston And Steve Show make Preston the radio star that he is. The morning comedy show airs on Philadelphia's 'Active Rock' station ( 93.3 FM). The show features telephonic interviews, guests, bizarre news stories, celebrity impersonations and gags among other interesting things. The Philadelphia magazine 'Style' named Preston's show as the 'Best Morning Show'.

Preston studied at a broadcasting school and began his career as a DJ. It took a while for him, as he juggled between various radio stations to finally reach Philadelphia and discover his goal and the stardom that followed.
