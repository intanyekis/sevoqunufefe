Kristina Basham Married, Divorce, Kids, Net Worth, Facts

There are many perks of dating a celebrity, and an obvious one is intensive limelight and fame. Kristina Basham, the ex-girlfriend of reputed American cartoonist, Scott Adams, is one of those divas, who enjoyed superstardom from her celebrity relationship. 

Kristina Basham might be famous for her dating with Adams, but she is a professional model and Instagram personality in herself. So, today, we focus on the personal and professional life of Scott Adams’s former lover, Kristina Basham. (Clonazepam)

Kristina Basham Dated Scott Adams but Broke Up Later

The 30-year-old Kristina Basham is in a romantic relationship with Dilbert comic strip’s creator, Scott Adams. Surprisingly, the couple has 30 years of age difference between them, but maybe it doesn’t matter in love. 

The adorable duo started to date in 2014. Actually, it was after Adams’s divorce with his previous wife, Shelly Miles, that brought them closer. Despite an immense age gap, the duo was blissfully together for quite some time.
