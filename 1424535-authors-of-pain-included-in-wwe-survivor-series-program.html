Authors Of Pain Included In WWE Survivor Series Program

A noteworthy name is in the WWE Survivor Series program.

Per SEScoops, the Authors of Pain (Akam and Rezar) are included in the program for the event. WrestleVotes had reported that they were told the program would include AOP and NXT’s Gallus. The program features the WWE roster, so their placement in it does not mean that the teams will be at WWE Survivor Series.

Fightful‘s Sean Ross Sapp responded to the WrestleVote report by noting that AOP have been “presumably signed” for a while, and he heard they were on travel plans in May. Sapp previously reported that WWE has considering a potential return for the duo, who were released in 2020, in August. Sapp had also reported in January that WWE discussed bringing them back.

WrestleZone will provide more information as it becomes available.
