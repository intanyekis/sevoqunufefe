Meet Jordan Poyers Wife Model Rachel Bush

American football star Jordan Poyer is a happily married man. He has been married to his wife, Rachel Bush, since February 2018.

And while he is a seasoned NFL star for the Buffalo Bills, his wife is a renowned model, with over 2.7 million followers on Instagram.

Rachel Bush Formerly Participated in Miss New York Pageant

The football’s spouse, Bush, was born on November 1, 1997, making her 24-year-old at present.

She grew up in Newcomb, New York, a very small town close to the Canadian border.

According to Post Star, a local newspaper, she applied for the Miss New York pageant after seeing an advertisement on Facebook.

Jordan Poyer's wife Rachel Bush during her Miss New York pageant days. (Photo: Facebook)

She was later invited to participate and finished as a semifinalist, gathering the exposure to help her get into modeling as a teenager.

The newspaper further reported that Bush and her younger sister, Jordan, were signed to Premiere Model Management in New Smyrna, Florida, shortly after the pageant.

She reportedly moved south to attend Florida Atlantic University in Boca Raton in the fall of 2015. The move also helped her to pursue her modeling career more actively.

Rachel Bush Is Now a Model and Entrepreneur

Bush finished her college education circa 2016.

In her June 2016 Instagram post, she even shared pictures stating she missed college, while adding she had found that “making money from Insta and photo shoots was more fun than class?”

After successfully launching her modeling career, she launched her line of Brazilian bikinis, Enjoy My Cake, in 2016. However, her first clothing line didn’t pan out as she had planned, and it was soon non-operational.

She is now running another beauty and skincare venture, LeaLa Natural Skincare, which is allegedly going smoothly.

In addition, she is also active on the OnlyFans platform and charges her subscribers $21 per month to “access behind the scenes photos” from her photoshoots.

Rachel Bush Was Linked to LeBron James

In the early days of her modeling career, Bush was linked to basketball star LeBron James.

According to online reports, the rumors of them dating each other swirled around the internet after James allegedly sent a message to Bush.

“He has a wife and two, three kids. He respects them, I respect him. They have certain people running their pages sometimes. You get DMed by multiple people,” she said on the Billy Madison Show, as per BET.

She continued to clarify that there was nothing between them, saying, “That has been the scenario, where their friends or their manager have tried to talk to me pretending to be them.”

Jordan Poyer and His Wife Rachel Bush’s Journey

Around the same time, Bush was also reported to be dating her now-husband Poyer.

Their relationship became official when they got engaged on Christmas Day in 2016. A few days later, on December 30, they also welcomed their baby girl Aliyah.

Since then, they have lived like a happy family, with Poyer often praising his wife for her motherhood.

“Words can’t explain this woman as a mother as a wife and as a friend … Aliyah is so dam lucky to call her, her mother. So caring so genuine so loving and she’s MINE .. can’t tell u how lucky I am ? Happy Mother’s Day my love?” he wrote in his 2018 post.

At present, they have been married for close to three years — and have been together for over five years.
