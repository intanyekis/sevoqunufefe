Al Roker Biography

Renowned as one of the top-paid American TV meteorologists, Albert Lincoln Roker Jr. is also a TV producer, journalist, author, writer, actor, presenter and voice actor, born on 20 August 1954, in Queens, New York City. He is best known for his role as the weatherman on “The Today Show” aired on NBC.

How wealthy is Al Roker as of early 2017? Authoritative sources have estimated his net worth to be over $30 million. This wealth has primarily been accumulated through his extensive career on various US TV networks, including hosting the famous show “Wake Up With Al”. Al is also recognized as the author of “The Morning Show Murders”.

Al Roker’s Total Worth $30 Million

In his early days, Al Roker aspired to become a cartoonist and was a member of the cartoon club in his high school. He later graduated with a BA degree in journalism and broadcasting from the State University of New York at Oswego after attending Xavier High School in Manhattan.

Before amassing his significant net worth, he worked as a weather forecaster on WTVH, previously known as When-TV. His net worth saw a significant rise after joining the “Today Show” on NBC in 1996.

Al Roker has gained fame through his successful show “Wake Up With Al”, which not only features weather forecasts but also covers international and American news, showcasing his talent as a TV show host.

On the film front, Al Roker made his TV debut in 1990, appearing as himself in “Another World”. His next appearance was in “Seinfeld” three years later. However, he has since starred in multiple films, including “Cloudy with a Chance of Meatballs 2” and “The Michael J. Fox Show”, all contributing to his net worth.

In his personal life, Al Roker has been married twice. His first marriage was to Alice Bell from 1984 to 1994, with whom he has a daughter. He has been married to American TV journalist from ABC News, Deborah Roberts, since 1995, and they have a daughter and a son.

However, Al Roker has faced health issues, including gastric bypass surgery and multiple knee operations. Despite this, he remains healthy and resides in New York City.
