Peddha Kapu Movie (Part 1): Cast, Story, OTT, Trailer, Songs, Release Date

Peddha Kapu is the new Telugu movie from Dwaraka Creations. Virat Karna and Pragati Srivastava play prominent roles. Along with the leads, the film also features Rao Ramesh, Naga Babu, Tanikella Bharani, Brigada Saga, Rajeev Kanakala, and Naren. The film is directed by Srikanth Addalaand produced by Miryala Ravinder Reddy. Chota K Naidu is the cinematographer, and Mickey J Meyer is the music director.

Release Date

Peddha Kapu Chapter 1 is scheduled to be released on August 18th, 2023.
