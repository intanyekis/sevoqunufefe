Kylie Jenner Steps Out With PartyNextDoor After Tyga Split

Tyga who? Kylie Jenner stepped out with PartyNextDoor on Wednesday, May 18, fueling rumors that she has moved on with the rapper following her recent split from Tyga. 

TMZ obtained photos of the two leaving the TCL Chinese Theatre in Hollywood on Wednesday night, where they took in Russell Simmons‘ All Def Comedy Live. The two didn’t hold hands but appeared to be friendly during their walk out.

This isn’t the first time Kylie, 18, and PND, 22, have spent quality time together recently. Just this past weekend, PND posted a photo on Instagram of his blinged-out Rolex displayed over Kylie Jenner’s glittering timepiece. “Diamond battles with @KylieJenner,” PND captioned the snapshot.

Us Weekly confirmed on May 12 that Kris Jenner‘s youngest daughter and her boyfriend had split again. “Kylie and Tyga are not speaking right now,” one insider told Us. Around that same time, Kylie retreated to her mother’s nest in Calabasas, California, where she snapped her return home. “I don’t know. I love really hard,” Kylie noted in one particularly vulnerable video. “I think that is enough from me for Snapchat tonight.”

Their relationship has been riddled with drama — Tyga shares a 3-year-old child with Rob Kardashian‘s pregnant fiancée, Blac Chyna — and cheating accusations.

PND (real name: Jahron Anthony Brathwaite) made headlines this past March after his ex-girlfriend, Grammy-nominated singer Kehlani Parrish, attempted suicide amid accusations that she had cheated on her boyfriend, NBA player Kyrie Irving, with PND.
