Edward Norton and Shauna Robertson Secretly Wed Before Son's Birth

Another surprise from one of Hollywood's most private couples! Not only are Edward Norton and longtime love Shauna Robertson the proud parents of a healthy baby boy, but the low-key twosome are also happily wed, a source confirms exclusively to Us Weekly. 

Norton, 43, and Robertson quietly got engaged back in 2011 following a six-year courtship, but the pair have actually secretly tied the knot back in 2012. No further details on the nuptials were available.

Robertson, a Canadian film producer who has worked frequently with Judd Apatow on comedies such as The 40-Year-Old Virgin and Knocked Up, gave birth to the couple's first child together back in March and the two are "thrilled and excited for parenthood," an insider told Us.

Us previously broke the news of Robertson's pregnancy back in March. A rep for the Moonrise Kingdom actor had no comment when contacted by Us.
