Madelaine Petsch Wiki Biography

Madelaine Grobbelaar Petsch was born on 18 August 1994, in Port Orchard, Washington State USA, of South African parents, and is an actress, best known for being a part of the CW television series entitled “Riverdale” playing the role of Cheryl Blossom. She’s been active in the industry since 2015, and all of her efforts have helped put her net worth to where it is today.
