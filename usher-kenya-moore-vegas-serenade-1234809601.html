Usher Serenades Kenya Moore, She Says He &quot;Snatched My Soul&quot;

Sundays are still for lovers even though Usher‘s residency is in its final days. As Bravo‘s biggest stars took over Sin City for BravoCon 2023, they naturally swung by the “hottest ticket in town” to see the King Of R&B and he turned Kenya Moore into his momentary muse.

When approaching the Real Housewives Of Atlanta star, Usher teased, “Oh girl, you got those hips out. They gonna be talking ’bout you tomorrow saying, ‘She put that thang on,’ baby. Yes, you did.” Moments later, he asked how she was doing and Moore gushed, “Great now.”

Ursher then got closer, “Oh, you great now?,” and repeated his question — sending onlookers into a frenzy. The two began to slow dance to his ballad, “Superstar.” Of the moment, Moore took to Instagram, reflecting, “Usher snatched my soul last night.”

The mother of one joins the list of ladies Usher has serenaded during his Las Vegas residency, which include Saweetie, Issa Rae, Gabrielle Union, Doja Cat, A’ja Wilson, and most notoriously, Keke Palmer.

His recent moment with Moore, though, once again had fans calling Usher’s partner, Jennifer Goicoechea, “number one on God’s list of strongest soldiers.”

One fan tweeted, “Usher’s wife is a strong lady” as others hilariously labeled him “a bonafide menace.” Yet, someone else commended the upcoming Super Bowl Halftime Show headliner‘s ability to stay in sultry moments while remaining aware of all boundaries.

They tweeted, “I actually want y’all to pay attention to Usher’s hands. Respectful and boundary aware. He is performing the fantasy without doing too much and, where necessary, subtly reinforcing his own boundaries.” They later doubled down, adding, “It’s why he’s such a staple in the industry. All the other R&B boys have such bad reputations because they cross lines constantly and the lack of good home training is palpable; He’s a class act.”
