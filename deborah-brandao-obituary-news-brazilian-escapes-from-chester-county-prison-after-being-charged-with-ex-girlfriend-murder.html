Deborah Brandao Obituary News: Brazilian escapes from Chester County Prison after being charged with ex-girlfriend murder.

In a daring escape, 34-year-old Danelo Cavalcante managed to evade the guards at Chester County Prison in West Chester on Thursday morning, just before 9 a.m. Astonishingly, he was spotted a mere hour later, nonchalantly strolling along a road in close proximity to the prison.

In a recent court ruling, it has been determined that Calvalcante has been found guilty of the heinous crime of killing Deborah Brandao, his girlfriend of two years. The tragic incident took place at her residence in Schuylkill Township back in April of this year.

Cavalcante reportedly expressed his dissatisfaction with her intention to reveal a murder charge that he had been confronted with in his home country of Brazil.

According to Chester County District Attorney Deb Ryan, it is noteworthy that Cavalcante is also being sought for a murder that occurred in Brazil in 2017.

According to authorities, the individual in question has been described as standing at a height of 5 feet (equivalent to 1.5 metres) and weighing approximately 120 pounds (which is equivalent to 54 kilogrammes). According to a photo released by the county, he is depicted with curly black hair and a closely-cropped beard and moustache.

In a message to the public, it is advised to remain vigilant and maintain situational awareness. In a world full of uncertainty, one can never truly discern the identity of the individuals who traverse alongside them.

Currently, there is no information available regarding the method by which he managed to escape.
